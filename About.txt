I'm building a very futuristic idea for the Government to address the issue of Waterlogging in the area or Roads during rainy seasons which causes major problem for the society and no strict major step has been taken yet thus I'm building a machine which will pull water through motors and will filter the water and keep the waste aside inside an storage specially designed for the plastic dust and etc and then the water will be filtered inside and will be passed through pipes to the other machine built similarly and all will be connected to a central machine working via automation through sensors and ai agents. The System will also have a manual access via website to the municipality or in charge for this machine. The machine will be placed right in the front face of the road and will pull the water directly from the streets and in some cases from the drains as the distance will increase so water pulling capacity will not function properly. The pipes which will be connected will be in layers and will pass through the electric poles at a certain distance above so that no human can malfunction it or disrupt the process. More over the water pressure will be monitored by a pressure sensitive sensor that would pass the signal to the other machines and eventually to the central machine placed right in front of ground or open pits therefore the chain system will pass the water to the central system and thus water will thrown to the required grounds or open pits and once the container storing the water in the machine is having normal water storage so again the passing of water flow will stop and again when the pressure sensor will trigger then process will start. There will be an flow sensor too kept within the pipe to analyse the flow of water is proper or not and this systems will operate through automation but could be manually operated too via the in charge in case the rain stops so to stop the machine without coming to the place directly. This is my core idea.
