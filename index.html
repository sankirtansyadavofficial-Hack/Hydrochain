Redefining Urban Water Management

A revolutionary AI-driven system designed to eliminate waterlogging from city streets during monsoon seasons — protecting lives, infrastructure, and the environment.

The Problem We Solve

Every monsoon season, cities across the nation face catastrophic waterlogging on roads and streets. Commuters are stranded, infrastructure is damaged, and health hazards multiply. Despite decades of awareness, no decisive technological solution has been deployed at scale.

HydroChain changes this. Our system places intelligent water-extraction machines at the front face of roads, pulling water directly from streets through high-capacity motors. The water passes through multi-stage filtration, separating plastic, dust, and debris into sealed waste containers, while clean water flows through an elevated pipe network connected at electric pole height — far from human interference.

Each machine is connected in a chain topology via pressure-sensitive and flow sensors, with all signals routed to a Central AI Hub that orchestrates the entire network. Operators can shut down machines remotely via a web dashboard — no physical presence required.

Intelligent Infrastructure

Every component of HydroChain is engineered for autonomous operation, real-time monitoring, and seamless coordination.

Automated Water Extraction

High-capacity motors positioned at road fronts pull water directly from streets and drains, activating automatically when pressure sensors detect waterlogging thresholds.

Smart Filtration System

Multi-stage inline filtration separates plastics, silt, and organic debris. Waste is stored in sealed compartments while clean water continues through the pipe network.

Pressure & Flow Sensors

Pressure-sensitive sensors at each node and flow meters inside pipes continuously monitor water levels, flow rates, and pipe integrity in real-time.

Chain-Connected Pipe Network

Elevated pipe layers run through electric poles at safe heights, connecting machine to machine in a chain topology that transports water to the central hub.

Central AI Control Hub

All machines route signals to a central system deployed near open pits or grounds. AI orchestrates the full chain — activating, throttling, and halting machines as needed.

Manual Override Dashboard

Municipal operators access a real-time web dashboard to monitor, override, or shut down any machine remotely — no physical presence at the site required.

How HydroChain Operates

From rain detection to water redistribution — a fully automated pipeline that protects your city in real-time.

01

Rain Detection & Activation

Pressure sensors at each machine detect rising water levels on the street surface. Once the threshold is reached, the system automatically activates the extraction motors.

02

Water Extraction & Intake

High-powered motors pull water from roads and nearby drains. Each machine is designed for maximum intake within a localized radius to ensure effective coverage.

03

Multi-Stage Filtration

Ingested water passes through filtration layers that separate plastics, silt, dust, and organic matter. Waste is stored in sealed containers for periodic collection.

04

Elevated Pipe Transport

Filtered water is pushed through pipe layers elevated on electric poles. The height keeps the system tamper-proof while maintaining consistent water flow between machines.

05

Chain Signal Propagation

Flow and pressure sensors relay data along the chain. Each machine communicates its status to the next, and all data converges at the Central AI Hub for real-time orchestration.

06

Central Redistribution

The Central Machine, positioned near open grounds or pits, receives all water from the chain and releases it into designated areas. Once container levels normalize, flow automatically halts.
